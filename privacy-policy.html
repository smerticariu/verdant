Verdant — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Verdant is a home-care app built to be calm and private. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app handles and what it does not do. The short version: Verdant has no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no servers of our own. Your information stays on your device and in your own private iCloud account.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. The developer of Verdant has no access to your information and operates no backend that receives it.

Data the app stores (on your device and in your private iCloud)

  • Your home setup and tasks — the rooms, cleaning tasks, completion history, streaks, and preferences you create in the app.
  • Before & after photos — optional photos you attach to a task. These are stored only on your device and in your private iCloud; they are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

All of this is saved locally and synchronized through Apple's CloudKit into your own private iCloud container. It is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and is not visible to the developer.

Subscriptions and payments

Verdant offers an optional auto-renewing subscription, "Verdant Premium" (weekly, monthly, or yearly). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We never see or receive your payment details. Billing, renewals, and cancellations are managed in your App Store account under Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification content is sent to or through any external server.

Third parties and tracking

Verdant contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Nothing you do in the app is tracked across apps or websites, and no data is sold or shared.

Children

Verdant is a general-audience productivity app and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Your control over your data

You can delete your data at any time from within the app ("Start over" in Settings) or by removing the app and its iCloud data from your Apple devices. Because we hold no copy of your data, deleting it on your device removes it entirely.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
